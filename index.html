THE INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON INNOVATIVE TRENDS IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
Kottayam, Kerala, India

Theme Of The Conference

Green Artificial Intelligence for a Sustainable Future

Green Artificial Intelligence (AI) for a Sustainable Future aims to harness the power of AI technology while prioritizing environmental sustainability. By integrating energy-efficient algorithms, optimizing computational processes, and considering the lifecycle of AI systems, Green AI seeks to reduce the carbon footprint associated with AI applications. It explores ways to develop environmentally-friendly AI models and applications that promote sustainability, making AI a driving force in addressing global challenges such as climate change, resource conservation, and sustainable development. Through its focus on responsible and eco-conscious AI practices, Green AI envisions a future where technology and environmental stewardship go hand in hand, creating a positive impact on both society and the planet.

The Conference

The 5th International Conference on Innovative Trends In Information Technology (ICITIIT'24) is an event covering all aspects of software and hardware designs of intelligent and smart computing systems. The conference is technically co-sponsored by IEEE Kerala Section. ICITIIT'24 allows attendees to benefit from various topics that cover state-of-art Information Technology research and development. ICITIIT'24 encompasses a wide range of topics covering Intelligent systems and applications, Big data processing, Internet of things and Blockchain, Secure information processing, Cloud computing, Edge and Fog Computing, Robotics and automation, Scalable computing, Neuromorphic engineering, Multimedia systems, Electronic Design Automation, Emerging technologies, Healthcare systems, Power and Energy Systems and many more. Accepted and presented papers will be submitted for possible publication in IEEE Xplore.

The Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam at Valavoor, Pala, Kerala is one among the IIITs that have been established as "Institutions of National Importance" by Ministry of Education, Govt. of India under the ambit of IIIT (PPP) Act 2017. The institute offers 4 year B.Tech. and B.Tech. (Hons) program, Ph.D. programs in frontier areas of CSE, ECE and Mathematics as well as M.Tech. in AI & Data Science, Cyber Security, CSE with specialization in Big Data& Machine Learning, CSE with specialization in Finance Technology for the working professionals.
